**Optional Ingredients**

!Top Recommended Ingredients!

Eggs/계란

Eggs are typically consumed poached or fried!
You probably want them yolk-y so that they add a great creaminess to your ramen

Green Onion/파

When you add green onion to your ramen, you give it
great visual appeal and also a nice hint of freshness!
It is a classic topping for most soup-based ramen

Fried Dumplings (or any of your favorite fried foods)/군 만두

Eating ramen with fried foods (dumplings, rice cakes, Korean pancakes, etc.)
really enhances your eating experience,
espcially with how they add amazing flavor

Cheese/치즈

Be sure to use cheap slices of cheese,
the iconic one being the squared American cheese packaged between plastic sheets!
Something about that toxic orange amtches perfectly with ramen

!Other Popular Ingredients!

Rice Cakes/떡

Boiled rice cakes add wonderful texture to ramen!
You can either use tube-shaped rice cakes called 떡볶기 떡 or
flat, circular rice cakes called 떡국 떡

Fish Cakes/어묵

Fish cakes offer more protein, and they come in call shapes and colors,
beautifying your ramen and providing great texture

Frozen Dumplings/어른 만두

Frozen dumplings serve a similar purpose as fried dumplings,
except they provide a different texture experience

Cooked Meats (ie. SPAM)/고기

Adding cooked meats in ramen is a quick way to get in more protein and some more flavor
