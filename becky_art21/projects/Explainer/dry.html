MAKE RAMEN
WITH ME WET DRY

Recommended Optional Ingredients

(highly recommended!) Egg
(highly recommended!) Fried Dumplings (or any of your favorite fried foods)

**Recommended Stovetop Ramen**

Indomie Goreng
Fire Noodles
Chapagetti

Insructions:

(Optional) Fry up an egg :D


Grab a small pot and fill it with water.
The amount doesn't necessarily matter, but
enough to approximately cover the noodles


Put the pot on stove and crank the heat up to HIGH!


Once the water boils, put in the noodle block
and dried vegetables (only if they are included)


Test the noodles every minute or so,
and once the noodles hit al dente DRAIN THE WATER!!!
Note: While it is recommended that around two tablespoons
of water are to be left in the pot, that isn't really important


Turn off the heat and bring you pot (with your noodles)
back and stir in your sauce packet


Add your fried egg/foods on top!


Enjoy!
