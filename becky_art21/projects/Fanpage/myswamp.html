what are you doing in my swamp

"What Are You Doing in my Swamp" is a meme taken from a scene in the first Shrek movie. Shrek screams this infamous line after discovering fairytale creatures finding refuge on his sacred swampland. In 2013, a Soundcloud musician, Tom M.C., released a techno song featuring this line. From there, the meme spiraled into remixes with displays of chaotic imagery and also a humorous response to people who feel as though some sort of their own territory has been trespassed.
