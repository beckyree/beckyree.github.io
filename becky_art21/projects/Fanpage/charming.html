prince charming

The Prince Charming meme is a recent development, with its origins in the third installment of the Shrek franchise. In this scene, Princess Cinderella betrays her royal female rebellion group for the romantic interests of Prince Charming, or Fairy Godmother’s son. Content creators on TikTok specifically have gotten many views by mocking Prince Charming’s iconic lines. Eventually, people began warping their face to replicate Prince Charming’s face structure and increase the humor in the short videos.
