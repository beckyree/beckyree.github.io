Enough Reggie

“Enough Reggie” began when Trumpet Guy, a content creator on the now defunct app, Vine, posted an edited version of a scene when the Inquisitor visits Shrek and Fiona. In the original scene, the Inquisitor is flanked by trumpeters. But after the initial fanfare, one trumpeter plays the Hawaii Five-O Theme. The original meme replaced the theme with Adele’s Hello, and while unfortunately that video has been removed, more people have gotten creative and made their own versions!
