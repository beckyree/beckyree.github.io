get shrekt

“Get Shrekt” is a meme based on wordplay. By combining the words “Get Wrecked,” written here as ‘wrekt,’ and “Shrek,” a phrase similar in meaning to other expressions like “get pwned,” “get destroyed,” etc. is formed. Originally the meme began when the account Battlecruiser Plays uploaded a Youtube video with the meme as the title, featuring a compilation of weird Shrek-related images and sounds. From then on, more videos came out with similar themes, and the phrase itself eventually earned a spot in the urban dictionary.
