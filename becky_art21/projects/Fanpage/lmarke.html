Lord Markiplier E

“Lord Markiplier E” goes by many other names, but “Lord Marquaad E '' and simply “E” are some common ones. During its time many people considered it the pinnacle of meme culture in that it made absolutely no sense. The meme began with a Twitter user posting an image of Lord Farquaad with Markiplier’s, a popular gaming Youtuber, face. From then on, the meme developed into pure chaos, with a simple caption, “E.” Why you may ask? No one knows.
